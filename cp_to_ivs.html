All Possible Level/IV Combinations + League Rankings

Table is sorted by from best to worst ranking in ANY league
GL: Great League (max CP 1500) UL: Ultra League (max CP 2500) ML: Master League (no max CP) % of MSP: What percent of the "Maximum Stat Product" does this IV give at the optimal level? Since TDO (Total Damage Output) is proportional to stat product, this essentially tells you how much damage output you are losing by using this IV compared to the rank 1 IVs. For example, 95% would mean you have 5% less TDO than the rank 1 IVs. How IVs are ranked: IVs are ranked by their "Stat Product" (attack x defense x stamina) at maximum level within each league, because stat product is proportional to Total Damage Output (TDO) which is the most important metric in PVP. This does not ALWAYS give the optimal IVs for every situation, but is how IVs are usually ranked.
